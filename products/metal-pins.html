Soft Enamel Pins

Raised metal lines with recessed enamel color. A strong choice for detailed artwork and cost-efficient bulk orders.

Hard Enamel Pins

Smooth polished surface with color filled level to the metal. Best for premium custom metal lapel pins.

Die Struck Pins

No enamel required. Relief metal, antique finish and polished edges give a classic badge-like look.
