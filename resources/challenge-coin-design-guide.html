Design guide

Designing custom metal coins and token coins.

Use this guide for custom metal coins, metal token coins, custom metal tokens and commemorative coins that need strong relief and durable finishes.

Separate manufacturing-intent searches from price searches

Many coin keywords are about gold, silver and bullion prices. A custom metal craft site should focus on coin design and manufacturing terms: custom metal coins, metal token coins, commemorative coins and custom metal tokens.

Plan the two sides

Most custom metal coins use one side for the logo or emblem and one side for text, date, serial number or event message. Keep small text away from the edge and avoid thin lines that cannot hold relief.

Choose relief and edge details

2D relief is clean and economical. 3D relief is better for portraits, mascots and sculpted symbols. Reeded, rope or wave edges make a metal coin feel more finished.

Packaging affects perceived value

Bulk token coins can ship in bags, while premium commemorative coins usually use capsules, velvet boxes, acrylic stands or printed presentation cards.
